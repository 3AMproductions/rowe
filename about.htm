Maren Roth and Nikki Portman are self-described fashion fanatics. From Maren's days working at New York Fashion PR powerhouse LaForce & Stevens to her position as PR Director for such brands as Paul & Joe, Lauren Moffatt and C&C California, her love of fashion and attention to detail has continued to help shape her own chic bohemian look. As a known indie hipster always up on the Rock N' Roll scene, Nikki has taken her love of music and intense passion for fashion and combined them, creating a rare blend of fashion forward style.

In March 2006 their individual styles come together to form a cohesive vision... one which makes up ROWE.
